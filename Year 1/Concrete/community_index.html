Summary

Community is the Brain Child of Dan Harmon and is produced by NBC from season 1 to season 4 (2009 - present) .

Community follows 7 quirky community college students from their first semester at Greendale Community College. They set up a study group to help learn spanish together, as their friendship develops so too do their adventures and story lines.
