Welcome to bronchaus.

This website is a trial and error based first attempt at getting to grips with..... internets.

I don't know at the moment whether this is going to be some sort of cultural blog (probably not), A portfolio website (likely) or some other new age attempt of bridging the gap between man and machine ever closer. Heralding a new age, typified by man and machine living as one and co-existing peacefully with fish & phish.

Join me on this meandering journey as we set sail for parts unknown. Hopefully we can all pick up a few nuggets of interest along the away or if not, we can all at least make up lies and tell people that the numbers crunch and the facts have figures.
