Abed Nadir

Abed Nadir (Danny Pudi) is a greendale student studying business and restaurant management because his father hopes he will one day want to take over the family falafel restaurant. Abed has other aspirations and hopes to one day become a director. Abed is an outsider and his only understanding of others emotions seems to come from pop culture references.
