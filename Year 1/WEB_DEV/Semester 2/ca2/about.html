ABOUT

Life is a new social experience. Life.com consolidates all current music, art, cinema & sports releases and information in to one website.

Life was created in 2013. Life focuses on Dublin City's best and brightest social outings. We here at Life hope to give you the reader a first glimpse at whats happening in the near future and what you can look forward to. We at Life like to think we know music, that's why we try to comprise the best and freshest acts on the music scene.
