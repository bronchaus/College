Jeff Winger

Jeff Winger (Joel McHale) is a former lawyer who was disbarred after being revealed his Law degree was doctored. He returns to Community College as his only option to regain the life he had. Jeff is an egotistical narcissist who doesn't wan't to engage with his peers but only to get his degree as smoothly as possible... through a series of events it is revealed Jeff has a lighter side and does in fact show consideration and affection towards his study group as the series progresses.
