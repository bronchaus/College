	<p>The LINC assists start-up companies in the region through our <a href="incubationsupport.html">Incubation/Office Space and Supports</a> and the provision of the<a href="http://www.newfrontiersprogramme.com/index.html"> New Frontiers Programme.</a> We are also invoved in a number of initiatives to support the development of an <a href="entculture.html">Entrepreneurial Culture at ITB</a>. </p>

	<p style="margin-top: 0; margin-bottom: 0;">The LINC has a range of bespoke training <u><a href="industrytrainingprog.html">Programmes Available</a></u> for industry. Have a look at our case studies to see how we are working closely with companies to meet their competence needs. </p>
</div>

	<p style="margin-top: 0; margin-bottom: 0;">The<a href="researchgroups.html"> <u>Research Groups</u></a> based in the LINC are involved collaboratively with research projects with industry, community organisations and different agencies in the locality. 	<p style="margin-top: 0; margin-bottom: 0;">The LINC has a wide range of facilities available for hire to local businesses and organisations. <u><a href="facilities.html">Rooms available</a></u> include boardrooms, meeting rooms, computer labs, lecture theatres and networking area. <u>Services available</u> include catering, IT infrastructure and audio visual equipment.</p>
