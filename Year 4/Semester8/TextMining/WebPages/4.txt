	            			<h2 class="headline"><a href="http://itbstudenthub.ie/?p=2450" rel="bookmark">Printing from your Office365 Student Email Account</a></h2> 
		                    		                        <a class="featureimg" href="http://itbstudenthub.ie/?p=2450" rel="bookmark"><img width="600" height="375" src="http://itbstudenthub.ie/wp-content/uploads/2015/12/outlook.jpg" class="attachment-post size-post wp-post-image" alt="outlook" srcset="http://itbstudenthub.ie/wp-content/uploads/2015/12/outlook-300x188.jpg 300w, http://itbstudenthub.ie/wp-content/uploads/2015/12/outlook.jpg 600w" sizes="(max-width: 600px) 100vw, 600px" /></a>
		                              
	                        <p><a target="_blank" href="http://itbstudenthub.ie/wp-content/uploads/articulate_uploads/Printing_from_your_email_account4/story.html">View tutorial</a> or follow these instructions:</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>Step 1: login to your ITB Student email account from <a href="http://www.outlook.com/student.itb.ie" onclick="__gaTracker('send', 'event', 'outbound-article', 'http://www.outlook.com/student.itb.ie', 'www.outlook.com/student.itb.ie');">www.outlook.com/student.itb.ie</a></p>
<p>Step 2: select New to compose a new email message.</p>
<p><a href="http://itbstudenthub.ie/wp-content/uploads/2015/12/1-acc.jpg"><img class="aligncenter wp-image-2451" src="http://itbstudenthub.ie/wp-content/uploads/2015/12/1-acc.jpg" alt="1 acc" width="590" height="290" srcset="http://itbstudenthub.ie/wp-content/uploads/2015/12/1-acc-300x147.jpg 300w, http://itbstudenthub.ie/wp-content/uploads/2015/12/1-acc-1024x502.jpg 1024w, http://itbstudenthub.ie/wp-content/uploads/2015/12/1-acc-900x442.jpg 900w, http://itbstudenthub.ie/wp-content/uploads/2015/12/1-acc.jpg 1600w" sizes="(max-width: 590px) 100vw, 590px" /></a></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>Step 3: address the email to <a href="mailto:mobileprint@itb.ie">mobileprint@itb.ie</a></p>
<p>No subject is required.  Click Attach from the menu</p>
<p><a href="http://itbstudenthub.ie/wp-content/uploads/2015/12/2-acc.jpg"><img class="aligncenter wp-image-2452" src="http://itbstudenthub.ie/wp-content/uploads/2015/12/2-acc.jpg" alt="2 acc" width="600" height="172" srcset="http://itbstudenthub.ie/wp-content/uploads/2015/12/2-acc-300x86.jpg 300w, http://itbstudenthub.ie/wp-content/uploads/2015/12/2-acc-1024x293.jpg 1024w, http://itbstudenthub.ie/wp-content/uploads/2015/12/2-acc-900x258.jpg 900w, http://itbstudenthub.ie/wp-content/uploads/2015/12/2-acc.jpg 1432w" sizes="(max-width: 600px) 100vw, 600px" /></a></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>Step 4: select a document from the <strong>OneDrive</strong> or <strong>Computer</strong> that you wish to attach</p>
